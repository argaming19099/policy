1. Information We Collect

AR Messenger is a lightweight client for accessing Google services. We collect and process:

  • Google User Data: Email address and profile name.
  • Gmail Content: Subject lines and messages (stored only in memory during use).
  • Local Metadata: A local token.json file stored in your AppData folder.

2. How We Use Information

Data is used only to provide core functionality: authenticating your identity via OAuth 2.0 and displaying/sending emails.

3. Data Storage

We do not use external servers. All data stays on your local machine. We never see or store your Google password.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google APIs. Your use is also subject to the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Contact & Deletion

You can revoke access by deleting the token.json file or revoking permissions in your Google Account settings.
